WebImgTool Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to WebImgTool. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your images and data.

1. No Server Uploads (Client-Side Processing)

The most important thing to know is that we do not upload your images to our servers. All image conversion, resizing, and processing happens locally within your web browser using JavaScript.

  • Your photos never leave your device.
  • We do not store, view, or share your files.
  • Once you close the tab, all data is cleared from your browser memory.

2. Data Collection

Since we do not require user accounts or logins, we do not collect personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment details

3. Third-Party Libraries

To provide features like PDF and HEIC conversion, we use trusted external libraries (CDNs). These libraries are loaded into your browser to help process files, but they do not collect your personal data.

4. Analytics

Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect basic, anonymous logs (such as IP addresses or browser types) to ensure the security and performance of the website. This data is not linked to your processed images.
